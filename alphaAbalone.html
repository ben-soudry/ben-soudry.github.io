AlphaAbalone - An Artificial Intelligence project using Minimax and Alpha-Beta Prunning

I created an artificial intelligence that plays the board game Abalone for my term project in 15-112,Fundamentals of Programming and Computer Science. The AI works by creating a decision tree of possible moves, in the same manner that computers play chess. Abalone has a higher branching factor than chess, which makes this game more challenging for AI. To see the code, click here: AlphaAbalone
